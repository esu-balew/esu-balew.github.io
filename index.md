## About

I am a QMUL based methods advisor (health economics) for the NIHR Research Support Service [(RSS) Hub](https://www.nihr.ac.uk/support-and-services/research-support-service/rss-hub-imperial) delivered by Imperial College London and Partners (Imperial College London, University College London, Queen Mary University of London and King’s College London).
